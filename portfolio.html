Portfolio

A collection of applications and websites I’ve worked on that have been developed and produced over the past two years. Many of them deal with how to make application hosting and development friendlier.

I led the user research and interaction design for each project, and collaborated with development and marketing teams to make the final product. Due to the confidential nature of my most recent work, I’ve replaced all sensitive information (people’s names, product names, and data) and modified the interface design where necessary.

  • Cloud Bridge

    A collaborative development tool that enables application development teams across the corporation to efficiently host and manage their code.

  • System Status Dashboard

    An enterprise dashboard that provides at-a-glance status information about business-critical applications.

  • Platform Engineering Website

    A website that introduces a cloud hosting provider by describing benefits of the open source technology, detailing available support, and consolidating useful documentation.

  • Insight

    An enterprise developer tool used for in-depth monitoring of application performance.
