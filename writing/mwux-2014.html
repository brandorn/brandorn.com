Notes from Midwest UX 2014

20 November 2015

Last fall I went to the Midwest UX conference in Indianapolis to get my design flow on. Despite being distracted by people-watching (Bloomington, Illinois, where I live, is near entirely devoid of the Warby Parker-, chambray-, chukka-wearing, Moleskine-toting kind) I managed to gather design nuggets from such excellent thinkers as Lorna Dunham (of the Mayo Center for Innovation), Peter Morville, Jared Spool, and Jon Kolko, and Christian Manzella.
