Hello over there.

I'm a product designer at Viget, focusing on user research, prototyping, and interface design.

As a designer, I take cues from the modernist design movement that emphasized close attention to form for the sake of content, prototyping early and often, and a practical familiarity with the methods and materials of production. I'm drawn to digital product design for the ways in which it blends longstanding disciplines. A good project draws on elements of information architecture and visualization, graphic design, and interaction design.

An Illinois native, I live in Durham with my wife, BethAnne, where I spend my free time in books and forests, sometimes in both. Though I studied English Literature and Economics, I’ve mostly been influenced by LEGO instruction manuals and Shel Silverstein poems. Email is a good way to reach me. Don't be a stranger.
