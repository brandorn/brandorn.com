Writing

Reflections on design, wonder, grace, and other affinities.

I once read an interview with author Vaclav Smil, who couldn't understand those academics who were what he called "drillers of deeper wells. These academics sit at the bottom of a deep well and they look up and see a sliver of the sky. They know everything about that little sliver of sky and nothing else. I scan all my horizons."

These are my attempt to scan the horizons and discover the connections. Because reality isn't a set of wells, but an intricate network of relationships.
