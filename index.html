Brandon Dorn is a user experience designer who believes that design can be an act of love.

Love for the work, love for the people who experience it. Currently I do the research and design of digital applications at State Farm. My projects involve user research, information architecture, interface prototyping, interaction design, and copy editing. Please see my resume [pdf] or request my portfolio if you’re interested in working together.

A native of the Prairie State, I live in central Illinois with my wife, BethAnne.
