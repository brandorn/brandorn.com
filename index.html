My name is Brandon Dorn, and I'm a user experience designer. I solve business problems with my mind and my heart.

My projects typically involve making sense of tangles of information. Currently I conduct user research and design digital applications at State Farm, where I put the principles of information architecture, interaction design, and copy editing to work. Please see my resume [pdf] or request my portfolio if you’re interested in working together.

A native of the Prairie State, I live in central Illinois with my wife, BethAnne.
