My name is Brandon Dorn, and I'm a user experience designer. I solve business problems with my mind and my heart.

I believe the success of any product is determined by how well it helps real people.

Currently I do user research and design in Durham, North Carolina, and have experience with information architecture, interaction design, and copy editing. Please see my resume [pdf] or request my portfolio if you’re interested in working together.

Born and raised in the Midwest, I live in Chapel Hill with my wife, BethAnne.
